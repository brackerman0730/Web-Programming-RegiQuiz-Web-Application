About Us

Why use RegiQuiz: Ever try putting questions into web applications like Quizlet or Kahoot but find that they are messy and hard to work with? RegiQuiz is for you! With RegiQuiz, you can go through flashcards that are formatted to answer math problems, rewriting code, and multi part questions. All of these card types can be in one flashcard set!
